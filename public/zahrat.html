about us

Discover. Play. Grow. Our Nursery Way!

Zahrat Al Nahda Nursery incorporates the Early Years Foundation Stage as a framework and is based on the latest international research on brain development in the early years.

Zahrat Al Nahda Nursery supports the emotional, social, cognitive, and physical development of each child. The nursery is conveniently situated in the Al Kawthar SBK Building, Al Khan and offers transportation services for families throughout Sharjah.

our education

Daycare (6 months to 2 years)

Our daycare program focuses on nurturing care and potty training for little ones. With gentle guidance and close supervision, we ensure a safe, comforting environment where infants and toddlers can grow, play, and develop essential early habits at their own pace.

Preschool (2 to 4 years)

Preschool at Al Nahda Nursery prepares children for formal schooling. Through fun learning activities, early literacy, and social interaction, we help develop focus, creativity, and independence, ensuring each child builds confidence and is ready for their next academic journey.

After School (4 years and above)

Our after-school care supports children with homework and provides engaging educational and recreational activities. It’s a space where kids can learn, relax, and grow after school hours—with caring staff always available for help and guidance.
